Market-testing a cloud venture idea

From wind parks to data centers

In this 12-week project, a German energy corporation asked us to validate an ambitious venture idea:

Powering energy-intensive cloud computing services directly with energy from their own wind and solar parks, and selling them to other businesses.

The project lead was pushing for an investment, while the other stakeholders saw too much uncertainty. Eva (lead innovation consultant) and I were brought in to enable a decision.

Big market, strong competition

70-90% of the 100 Bn. € European cloud market is dominated by the seemingly invincible hyperscalers AWS, Azure and GCP. But the outlook that only 1% of the non-hyperscaler market are worth 100 Mio. €, was sufficient as a perspective to pursue this opportunity.

1% of the non-hyperscaler market is worth 100 Mio. €

Hypotheses & alignment on a goal

In order to be successful, the venture idea needed to fulfill the following 3 high-level hypotheses:

1. Feasible: We are capable to provide this service

Out of scope for this project, as the client's project lead explored the idea's feasibility in depth before this project.

2. Viable: We can offer this service for an attractive price

To validate this element, Eva collaborated with an external business analyst to calculate a business case with different scenarios.

3. Desirable: We can convince customers to buy the cloud service

To validate this element, we conducted a fake door test: We created a landing page prototype to test how attractive the service would be to real prospects.

In the project kickoff, we agreed with our stakeholders on the goal of 3 customer letters of intent (LOI): If we managed to get 3 customers declaring their purchase interest in a LOI, we would perceive the desirability to be validated.

Research: Who is buying, and why?

To use the right keywords in our test, we researched which value propositions our competitors used to market their cloud services. While value propositions using one or two of the values sustainability, high performance, and EU-sovereignty are already occupied, the combination of the three values seemed attractive for our positioning.

A combination of 3 value propositions for a niche in the market

Customer segments & buyer profiles

To target the right people with our advertisement budget, we developed a set of customer segments and buyer profiles, utilizing the project lead's experience and supporting desk research.
