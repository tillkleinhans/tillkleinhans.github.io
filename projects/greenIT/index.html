Market-testing a cloud venture idea

(subject to NDA – content generalized)

Situation

Inside an energy corporation, our main stakeholder was developing a venture idea in the cloud computing industry for more than 5 years now, and was pushing for building the venture. For the other stakeholders, the investment was still too uncertain.

Challenges:

  1. Client project lead fixed on idea for a long time
  2. Complex topic
  3. High expectations: Deliver a foundation for an 10M€ investment decision within 12 weeks

Task

In this short 12-weeks project, Futurice was asked to validate the venture's desirability and viability, and enable an investment decision.

Eva focused on the viability part, while I focused on the desirability validation.

Methods & structure

Desirability:

The client conducted qualitative interviews with hypothetical questions ("would you buy XYZ") before. We chose a fake door test to validate if customers would buy the intended offering. This way, we could get unbiased results from real customers.

  • Alternatives?
  • Negatives? Wrong channel for buying B2B cloud computing services

Preparation, desk research

  • Understand tech topic: IaaS, PaaS, competitive landscape
  • Compare venture idea w/ competition → Unique value propositions
  • Ansprache: Create customer segments to whom the USPs should be relevant

Experiment design

  • Test 2 unique value propositions → 2 different ads, subpages for more info
  • Test 3 cloud computing offerings (= different hardware that needs to be bought)
  • Form in the end, 2 versions for 2 different segments
  • What to measure: Path through site, CTRs for the ads
  • Success: 3 letters of intent (LOI)
Include images:
  1. Wireframes – gesamter Flow
  2. (Wireframe sections? Ads → Website, Website + Unterseiten + Formular, Calendly & weiterer Prozess)

UI / website design

  • Brand → Font, visual design
  • Very short time, 2 weeks → Focus on most important elements, prototype the rest
  • Limited visual assets = hi-res energy infrastructure photos → amend with dot patterns, isometric illustrations, icons to create consistent brand
Include images:
  1. Brand Koordinatensystem + positioning
  2. Visual elements: hi-res photos + isometric illustrations, icons + dot pattern to connect & create coherent visual appearance

Results & interpretation

  • 12 contact form submits, 0 qualified / answering
  • (can I find the funnel?) 3000-4000 € budget? 100k impressions? 90-120 visits? Was stimmt hier nicht?
  • Ergebnis klar negativ, user paths / flows nicht ausgewertet
  • Hypothese falscher Kanal statt undesirable offering → Qual. interviews auf Fachmesse → Nicht desirable, nicht unique ggü. competition

Pivot

  • Colocation statt IaaS → viel leichtere Competition da keine Hyperscalers, näher an eigener Kompetenz (Energiekonzern)
  • Durch co-created experiment & Ergebnisse buy-in from client project lead
